About Ark Racer

About My Nickname

I first started using the name "Ark Racer" when I was roughly 10 years old. I can't remember exactly how I came up with that name, but I think it was derived from content found in two separate Nintendo DS games. I likely got the word "Ark" from Raiders of the Lost Ark, one of the three films recreated in LEGO Indiana Jones: The Original Adventures, and I likely got the word "Racer" from "racing game," the genre into which Mario Kart DS falls. Although this significance has been mostly lost, the name "Ark Racer" will always be close to me.

About My Favorite Number

My favorite number is 25. There are several reasons for this:

  • My birthday is September 25.
  • My birth year is 2005; 2005 without zeroes is 25.
  • The nearest liturgical Sunday to my birthday is the 25th Sunday in Ordinary Time.
  • I will be 25 years old on my Golden Birthday.
  • Pikachu is my favorite Pokémon, and Pikachu's National Pokédex Number happens to be 25.
